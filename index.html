Mr. Moses Oduro Boateng

ORDER OF THE VOLTA COMPANION

A brief timeline of Mr. Boateng's life:

  • 1952 - Born in Ghana at Koforidua
  • 1974 - Became the president of the National Union Of Ghana Students(NUGS)
  • 1975 - Graduated the University of Cape Coast (Ghana) with a Bachelor of Arts (Education) Degree
  • 1976 - Employed by Barclays Bank. Where he held many managerial positions and excelled in all fields.
  • 1988- Obtained an MBA at Glasgow, Scotland
  • 2001- Joined UniBank as Deputy Managing Director
  • 2002- Appointed as Managing Director of the Ghana National Petroleum Commission.
  • 2014- dies at age 52.

After retirement from GNPC, he established and operated a number of private businesses, notable among them being Koforidua Guest Hotel. In his entire career as an employee and also as a private businessman. Yaw Boateng (as called by close family members) was extremely hardworking and due to his passion for success he was able to achieve outstanding results. He devoted himself to work with a very rare sense of responsibility while committing himself in every way to the satisfaction of stakeholders.
